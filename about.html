ABOUT FINSPHERE GLOBAL

A free, independent financial education platform built to make tax and money concepts understandable for everyone — wherever you live in the world.

OUR MISSION

NewFinera was created to solve a simple problem: financial and tax information is often scattered, jurisdiction-specific, jargon-heavy, or locked behind paywalls. We believe everyone deserves access to clear, accurate, free educational resources to understand how their money and tax system actually works.

We built free tax calculators covering Australia, the United Kingdom, the United States, Pakistan, and the UAE, alongside in-depth educational guides on income tax, superannuation, cryptocurrency taxation, and the growing role of artificial intelligence in personal finance.

WHO'S BEHIND FINSPHERE GLOBAL

NewFinera is operated by SS Traders Education, founded by an ACCA-qualified finance professional with a background in audit and financial compliance across manufacturing, retail, and service industries. Our content is written and reviewed with a strong emphasis on accuracy, referencing official government sources including the ATO, HMRC, the IRS, and Pakistan's FBR.

Important: While our founder holds professional accounting qualifications, NewFinera operates strictly as an educational publisher. We do not provide licensed tax agent services, financial advice, or any regulated financial service through this Site. For personalised advice, always consult an appropriately licensed professional in your jurisdiction.

HOW WE KEEP CONTENT ACCURATE

Tax rates and rules change regularly — often annually through government budget announcements (Australia's Federal Budget, the UK's Spring/Autumn Budget, US IRS inflation adjustments, and Pakistan's Finance Act). We review and update our calculators and guides to reflect current published rates, and we clearly date all content so you know how current it is.

OUR EDUCATIONAL PARTNERS

We reference and link to respected, independent financial education resources including Investopedia, Coinbase Learn, Binance Academy, BabyPips, Morningstar, and FX Street — connecting our readers to authoritative information beyond what we cover directly.
